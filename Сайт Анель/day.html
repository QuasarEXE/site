The head of state received the Minister of Justice Azamat Eskarayev

By Yedilova Anel 04.10.23


Kassym-Jomart Tokayev heard a report from the Minister of Justice Azamat Eskarayev on the results of the work of the justice authorities for nine months of this year and on plans for the coming period



Azamat Eskarayev reported on the legislative measures being taken, in particular, on the draft laws developed on the optimization of the Criminal, Criminal Procedure and Criminal Executive Codes, the Code of Administrative Offences, as well as on improving and expanding the scope of administrative justice. He also informed about innovations in the areas of civil registration, real estate rights, notarial, advocacy and enforcement proceedings, and measures to protect intellectual property rights. The President instructed the Minister to work closely with the Prosecutor General’s Office to promptly adopt by-laws to implement the Law “On the return of illegally acquired assets to the state.” Kassym-Jomart Tokayev also noted the need to ensure coordinated work with interested government bodies and compliance with the requirements of the Constitution when adopting laws and regulations
